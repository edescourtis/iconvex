The exact lexical domain selected here has 63,010 mappings: five structural
markers, 500 numbers, the null symbol, and 62,504 ordinary symbols. Coordinates
are lexical pairs of numbers. The codec converts complete lexical tokens; the
full-sign grammar is validated by a separate API.
