An example of conversions that involve the IBM i specific CCSID 61175:
  • from CCSID 1175 to CCSID 61175: ‘FC’x to ‘7F’x
  • from CCSID 1175 to CCSID 13488: ‘FC’x to ‘0022’x
  • from CCSID 61175 to CCSID 13488: ‘7F’x to ‘0022’x
